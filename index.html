Powering Vietnam's Future

With Vietnam’s emergence as an economic powerhouse the country’s energy needs are exploding. Chan May is answering this call through the creation of a safe, secure and scalable source of energy.

With an eye towards supporting Resolution No. 55-NQ, Chan May is working hand-in-hand with the Vietnamese government in creating the first independent LNG power project in central Vietnam. Located in the heart of Vietnam, Chan May LNG Joint Stock Company is a US majority owned power project that will provide on average 24-25bn kWh per year into the central Vietnamese economy. The project promises to create breakthroughs in the domestic energy sector, stimulate vigorous job creation and economic growth in post-Covid era, and contribute to the growth of trade and investment relations between Vietnam and the USA.
